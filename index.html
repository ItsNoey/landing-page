When to Visit

I highly recommend that you visit Thailand anytime from November to February. By November, it is usually not rainy season anymore. But, in 2022, we still had to deal with some rain. Climate change, am i right?

Where to Visit

Indeed, I am Thai, but I am still a stranger to many parts of my country. I can say that you should give a few days to Bangkok. Stay in the Thonburi or Sathorn area, if you want to visit the temples and art scenes. Stay in Sukhumvit, if you want to be near all the convenience and city vibe. It can be overwhelming, just fyi. Other than that, I am quite useless.
